Vedic & Astrometric Computations

1. Drik Siddhanta & Zero Dependencies
This dashboard relies on zero external APIs. It calculates precise orbital mechanics entirely client-side using algorithms by Jean Meeus, with full Equation of Time terms and multi-term lunar perturbations. Lahiri Ayanamsa is applied for sidereal Nakshatra positions.
2. Tithi & Nakshatra Mechanics
A Tithi advances exactly when the angular distance between the Sun and Moon increases by 12 degrees. A Nakshatra changes as the sidereal Moon passes through 13°20' segments of the zodiac. The Moon's longitude includes perturbations for evection, variation, annual equation, and other major terms.
3. Dinamana (Daylight) & Kaalams
Daylight (Dinamana) is strictly the span between local Sunrise and Sunset. This span is divided into 8 mathematical segments to accurately map Rahu, Yama, and Gulika Kaalams based on the weekday.
