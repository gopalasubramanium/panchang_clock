Vedic & Astrometric Computations

1. Drik Siddhanta — Zero Dependencies
All calculations are performed entirely client-side using Jean Meeus algorithms with 22-term lunar perturbations and Lahiri Ayanamsa for sidereal positions.
2. Tithi & Nakshatra End Times
End times are computed by iteratively scanning forward to find angular threshold crossings, with bisection refinement.
3. Amrit Kaalam
Amrit Kaalam is from the Choghadiya system. Day/night divided into 8 segments, Moon-ruled segment identified by weekday.
4. Dinamana & Kaalams
Daylight span divided into 8 segments for Rahu, Yama, Gulika mapping by weekday.
